Hi,   I'm Igor


I am a Product Designer and UX expert with more than 7 years of experience, living in New York City. I Have experience designing digital and physical products. The best part of what I do is finding solutions to complex problems that then make life easier for others. My mission is finding a compromise between business needs and user expectations.

When I’m not hard at work I enjoy skiing, playing around with the newest technologies, and read Medium

I would like to learn more about your project.

If you are a non-profit organization please feel free to reach out to me as well; I would be happy to help you for free.
